The India Savings Awareness Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze the financial saving habits of Indians and promote awareness about investment options like SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) and SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan).

This project combines data analysis, dashboard design, financial insights, and Excel automation to transform raw survey data into meaningful business insights that can help individuals, financial advisors, and organizations understand savings behavior and encourage better financial planning.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze the current savings habits of Indian citizens.
Identify the major barriers preventing people from saving money.
Measure awareness of SIP and investment opportunities.
Educate users on the difference between SIP and SWP.
Provide a simple Budget Planner and SIP Calculator to encourage financial planning.

📈 Key Insights
64% of surveyed individuals have little or no monthly savings.
Average monthly savings are approximately ₹4,200.
Only 38% of respondents are aware of SIP investments.
More than half of the respondents are willing to begin investing with proper financial guidance.
The biggest barriers include low income, lack of financial education, and uncertainty about where to start.
🚀 Why This Project Matters

Financial literacy remains a major challenge in India. This dashboard demonstrates how data analytics can uncover financial behavior patterns and support data-driven awareness campaigns. It showcases practical dashboard development skills while solving a real-world problem.
